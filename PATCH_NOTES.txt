EMBERHOLD — PATCH NOTES
=======================

Versioning: numbered releases (0.100, 0.101, ...) mark playtest milestones.
Interim fixes append letters (0.101a, 0.101b, ...) until the next number.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
v0.101 — playtest patch 1
----------------------------------------------------------------------
HEROES
- Kaelis (Blade) nerfed: attack 15 (was 18), critical 5 (was 20),
  attack speed 14 (was 16).
- Fenwick: Quickdraw reduced to +50% attack speed (was +60%).
- Syrene: Cinder Nova now applies BURN — a stacking damage-over-time
  that lasts the whole fight. Each cast adds 2 damage per second.
  Burn is tracked per-source, so future burn effects can deal
  different damage rates (and enemies will eventually burn heroes).
- NEW HERO: Aldric the Paladin — balanced tank/damage/support.
  Consecration: party +50% defenses for 4s and heals the lowest-HP
  ally 15.
- Liora is now titled PRIESTESS; Sanctuary unchanged (heal party 15).
- Thornwild: Wildkin Bloom's heal is now REGROWTH — the lowest-HP
  ally heals 6/sec for 3s (18 total, slightly more than Sanctuary
  per target) instead of an instant 15.

RELICS
- Phoenix Feather returns: auto-revives a fallen hero at 30% HP
  during combat OR after a damaging event. Found in every region's
  shrine pool and stacks up to 3 per run.
- Combat relics slightly reduced in all regions: Ashen Fang +12%,
  Warding Idol -12%, Hourglass of Sol 15%, Crystal Lens +10%,
  Spelunker's Lantern +5, Vein of Quartz +4, Stalker's Eye +6,
  Echoing Charm 12%, Wolfsblood Vial +15%, Ironbark Hide +3,
  Hunter's Eye +6, Tanglevine Charm -12% (also reworded).

EQUIPMENT
- Each region's boss now has a 3-item pool (9 single-modifier items
  total): Marsh adds Gloamward Veil (+5 MDef), Cave adds Crystalline
  Sight (+6 Crit), Forest adds Huntmark Talisman (+6 Acc).
- Waygate text clarified: "Only bosses can drop equipment."

MAP & ENCOUNTERS
- Waygates, shrines, and events are spaced out: the same type never
  appears in back-to-back columns, different types back-to-back are
  rare (~15%). Special counts reduced to 2 waygates + 2 shrines +
  2 events (plus the final waygate). Paths hold notably more enemy
  encounters (combat density ~73%, was ~63%).
- Events never repeat back-to-back within a region.
- Event combat buffs reduced (values roughly halved); two buff
  events now give gold/healing instead.
- Boss reinforcements: the Hollow Sovereign has a 10% chance of two
  basic-enemy adds and 25% chance of one; the Herald of Gloaming has
  a 10% chance of one.

UI
- Stat cards are mobile-first: tap-to-toggle everywhere, larger tap
  targets, tap the open card to dismiss. The Hold's stat icon sits
  inline after the hero description (no longer overlaps attack speed).
- Two new descent modifiers: Vengeful Spirits (enemies +10% crit) and
  Stoneskin Blight (enemies +25% defenses).

----------------------------------------------------------------------
v0.100 — first playtest draft
----------------------------------------------------------------------
- 3 regions in the Earthen Realm (Forgotten Marshland, Hollowed Deep,
  Ogre Forest) with region power scaling 1x / 2x / 2.5x.
- 6 heroes with tap-to-cast ultimates charged by attacks.
- Branching Slay-the-Spire-style maps, guaranteed mid-run waygates,
  stacking descent modifiers, region-specific boss equipment, relics,
  events, boss-kill recovery, hero benching, treasure caches.
- Full save persistence (localStorage), procedural ambient music with
  volume control, post-fight combat review with Simple/Detailed log.
- Campaign balance simulator at tools/simulate.js.
